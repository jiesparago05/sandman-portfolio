How It Works

The Commission Process

A clear, collaborative process from first idea to finished figure — with progress updates at every stage.

01

Send Your Idea

Share your concept and reference photos — the figure, the character, the look you're after.

02

Review & Assessment

I review the figure, scale, and complexity to scope out exactly what the build will take.

03

Quote & Timeline

You get a clear quotation and an estimated timeline before any work begins.

04

Down Payment

A down payment confirms your build slot and locks in the schedule.

05

Work-in-Progress Updates

I send progress photos throughout so you're part of the build the whole way.

06

Final Photos & Approval

Final showcase photos for your approval before the piece is sealed and finished.

07

Pickup, Delivery or Shipping

Carefully packed and handed off — local pickup, delivery, or safe shipping.

The Artist

About Dave Bedia

I'm Dave Bedia — a custom toy designer based in Quezon City, Philippines. My journey into art toys and designer toys started in 2018, and it's where I get to freely express my creative side.

Most of my work is mecha-themed, inspired by movies, anime, and racing cars. There’s nothing like watching an idea become an actual figure you can see and hold — that moment when the inner voice goes “Yes, I’ve done it… what’s next?” is exactly why I keep building.

Signature Style

Mecha-themed designs inspired by movies, anime, and racing cars — with clean lines and a collector-grade finish.

Tools & Materials

Airbrush & acrylics, hand-detailing, panel lining, primers and protective topcoats.
