ABOUT SANDMAN

I'm a custom toy artist specializing in hand-painted repaints, kitbashing, and full custom builds of art models and action figures.

Every piece I create starts with a conversation — your vision, your favorite character, your dream design. From there, I bring it to life using professional-grade airbrush equipment, high-quality paints, and years of experience working with different scales and materials.

Whether it's a detailed Gundam repaint, a one-of-a-kind character custom, or a full scratch build — I treat every project like a work of art, because that's exactly what it is.

Every piece is unique. Every piece is built for you.
